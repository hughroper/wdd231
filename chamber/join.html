Membership Levels

Non Profit Membership Level

Non-Profit Membership is available to qualifying charitable, educational, and community service organizations. This membership helps local non-profits connect with businesses and residents while promoting their mission.

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership is an affordable option for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations looking to establish a presence within the chamber and connect with the local business community.

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership is designed for businesses that want increased visibility and additional opportunities to promote their products and services within the community.

Gold Membership Level

Gold Membership is our premier membership level, offering maximum visibility, promotional opportunities, and access to exclusive chamber benefits.

Non-Profit Membership

Annual Cost: No Fee

Benefits

  • Basic chamber directory listing
  • Invitations to chamber networking events
  • Access to chamber training workshops and educational resources
  • Ability to submit community announcements for chamber publications
  • Participation in selected chamber programs and community initiatives

Ideal For

Charitable organizations, schools, community groups, and other non-profit entities seeking visibility and community connections.

Bronze Membership

Annual Cost: $100

Benefits

  • Basic chamber directory listing
  • Access to chamber networking events and luncheons
  • Participation in chamber training workshops and educational programs
  • Inclusion in chamber newsletters and member communications
  • Opportunity to promote business announcements through chamber channels
  • Eligibility for member-only event discounts

Ideal For

Small businesses, startups, sole proprietors, and organizations seeking affordable exposure and networking opportunities while building community connections.

Silver Membership

Annual Cost: $250

Benefits

  • All Non-Profit Membership benefits
  • Enhanced directory listing with business description and logo
  • Featured placement in chamber newsletters and publications
  • Social media promotion through chamber channels
  • Discounts on chamber-sponsored events and training programs
  • Eligibility for periodic business spotlights on the chamber website homepage
  • Priority registration for chamber networking events and luncheons

Ideal For

Growing businesses seeking greater exposure, professional development opportunities, and stronger community engagement.

Gold Membership

Annual Cost: $500

Benefits

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium placement in the online member directory
  • Increased opportunities for homepage spotlight features
  • Additional social media promotions throughout the year
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities at chamber events
  • Enhanced advertising opportunities in chamber publications
  • Greater discounts on chamber events, training programs, and luncheons
  • Recognition as a Gold Member at chamber functions and community events

Ideal For

Established businesses and organizations seeking the highest level of visibility, marketing support, and community leadership opportunities.
