About Me

Hello. My name is Hugh Roper, I'm the inventor of Teslug, an aspiring horticulturalist and I prefer green chili (versus red chili). I enjoy hiking outdoors, especially because White Rock Canyon, Bandelier Monument, and the Valles Caldera National Preserve are close by. A hobby of mine is learning about long range (LoRa) inexpensive radios that work with Meshcore, an open source, off-grid, decentralized, mesh network built to run on affordable, low-power devices. I also enjoy gardening and learning landscape architecture.
