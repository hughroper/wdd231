Michele Roper

Michele is a classical harpist shaped by the Salzedo tradition and refined through conservatory training with Alice Chalifoux. She creates beauty and peace through both performance and instruction, guiding students with disciplined technique that leads to expressive artistry. As a performer, she brings a polished, refined presence to solo, ensemble, and orchestral settings across the region. In the Los Alamos arts community, she is known for her collaborative spirit, reliability, and dedication to classical craft. Through her music and teaching, she preserves the Salzedo tradition and inspires the next generation of harpists.
